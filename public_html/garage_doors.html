GARAGE DOORS

Jaeger Lumber’s Overhead Door Division has been serving our local communities since the 70's. Clopay is the brand we have chosen and we did so because they have some of the best overhead doors on the market. Clopay has recently added an entire line called Holmes, which produce beautiful carriage style doors.

Jaeger Overhead Doors is committed to the highest standards in workmanship and safety. Our installers are employees, not subcontractors, so that every service call, be it commercial or residential, will be performed to our high standards. In fact, Jaeger Overhead Door’s crew is considered to be the most preferred ‘subcontractor’ among builders, when it comes to garage door installation!
