Welcome to our kitchens area


Well crafted kitchen cabinets can beatify your home as well as provide reliable, usable storage space for many years to come.



Our focus is to provide you, your architect and your builder with a professional and welcoming environment to discuss your window, entry door, interior door, kitchen and bath cabinetry, countertops, flooring, moulding and millwork needs. Our store consists of 2 seasoned kitchen/bath designers and 2 window/door/millwork specialists eager and willing to assist you with your design options. Feel free to come in and browse our 3,800 square foot store at your convenience! We’ve also created an office within the store to accommodate a meeting with your architect complete with drafting table, computer, phone, etc. Choosing the finishes for your home should be enjoyable; we’d like to think we’ve built a showroom that makes the shopping experience more comfortable.
