Welcome to Jaeger Lumber

Jaeger Lumber has been serving our local communities since 1937. We are a family owned and operated business consisting of 7 yards and one design showroom.

Over the years we have evolved from a home center to a builder-oriented lumberyard to better serve our customer base.

We pride ourselves in providing the high level of service and expertise that the custom homebuilder and remodeler needs. We excel in providing the best millwork solutions to our builders and for those that still have the time to “Do-It-Yourself”. 

Jaeger Lumber’s greatest strength lies with our employees, most have been with us for many years. We do not have the employee turnover of the chain stores and that is what keeps our customers coming back.

Our employees work for our customers as much as they work for Jaeger Lumber.

Jaeger Lumber offers more than just the products we sell, we offer you our experience!

Our buyers are always searching for the best products in every category for our market areas.

We always stock the best quality to ensure our custom builders keep coming back. Our hardware and tool departments have also been designed for the professional builder where quality is a must!

Our inventory mix is catered to our local market areas but we do ship out of alternate locations when a product is not stocked locally.
Despite our growth and increasing market share, we still consider ourselves your neighborhood lumber yard.
