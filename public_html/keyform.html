May Survey

Dear Customers:

We are facing decision time here at Jaeger over holding our Key Account trip. Business through April has been disappointing but as someone reminded me, when things are bad, don’t give up programs that may help business. We have held trips since 1988 and believe they do benefit you and us. Based upon that determination, we ask that you give us some indication of your interest in participating. Please indicate one of two destination choices and the third choice is skip a year. Let me remind you that even if you cannot earn enough points, most customers do pay something towards their trip. You also have three years as remodelers to use them before the earliest year is lost.

We have one proposal for the Palma Real Resort in the Dominican Republic. The second one is a return to 2010’s Wyndham Sugar Bay in St. Thomas. The beaches in the Punta Cana area of Dominican Republic are spectacular, rooms beautiful, free golf, food more interesting, better than St. Thomas; the view, the town and trip options better in Sugar Bay. Both are all inclusive. Air travel time is about the same. Both are safe!

Which would you prefer?
Palma Real Resort, Domican Republic Wyndham Sugar Bay, St Thomas Don't hold a trip this winter.

Several of you have never travelled; most have at least once. No one is ever charged more for material because of our program, in fact those of you selected to join are our favored customers; we, including me, look to you for all our business indicators. Should we cancel the program?


If you appreciate our program, some comments please-


Our company approach is to have more locations serving a close in business area. Convenience for you and for us in serving you is part of the profile. A few yards choose large central locations, and there aren’t many left. They offer some economy of scale, more inventory in one place but not so pleasant for customer pickups, poor if any showrooms, dispatchers instead of more trained people to talk to. That’s our perception but maybe you would like us to offer a more streamlined approach. Union and Lakewood can offer that; our small sites cannot. Any suggestions?
