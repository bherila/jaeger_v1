Company Feature Description
Full Custom Custom Wood Products (CWP) is Jaeger Lumber's premier cabinet line. Let your imagination run wild and CWP will make your dreams come true with over 100 door styles and five wood species available to choose from. This full custom line will turn your project into a showpiece.
Semi Custom Jaeger Lumber offers Diamond Cabinets as one of our semi-custom cabinet lines. Utilizing 4 wood species, and many available product modifications, Diamond will deliver cabinetry in as few as two to four weeks. Diamond's varied product line will accommodate any budget.
Quick Availability Cabinetry Jaeger Lumber offers you the versatility of a 3-5 day lead time with over 25 different door styles available to you with Aristokraft. With Aristokraft cabinetry, Jaeger Lumber can coordinate your new kitchen from design to delivery in as few as 10 working days.
Semi Custom Wellborn is a family owned cabinet company that focuses on customer satisfaction and timely production. This is a line typically used by the custom builder and praised by the remodeler. Please visit their website for more information. Click on the logo or picture above. We have great faith in the quality and versatility of this line.
Semi Custom Fieldstone is a well known brand of cabinets that services the semi custom range of projects. They are a welcome addition to most of our showrooms. Fieldstone caters to the higher end side of Semi custom. This is currently our fastest growing line.
Semi Custom Imperia cabinets offer a wide range of cabinet styles but we typically use them for their contemporary design. We can offer that sleek and modern look with Imperia. While not a popular style in our area, if its contemporary design you desire Imperia is the best match we can offer.
