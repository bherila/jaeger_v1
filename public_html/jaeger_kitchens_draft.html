Welcome to our kitchens area

Well crafted kitchen cabinets can beautify your home as well as provide reliable, usable storage space for many years to come.

Jaeger Kitchens is a division of Jaeger Lumber and can be found in 7 of our 8 locations.  We have up to date showrooms and talented designers at each location.  Several of our designers have obtained their CKD certifications but all of them are excellent and have been with us for years.  Feel free to drop by any of our design centers for a quick tour or make an appointment so we can give you our undivided attention. We are constantly updating our displays in order to keep up with today's design trends.

Jaeger Kitchens has been selling to the trade for years and also welcomes home owners that are planning their first remodel.  We are advisors, designers, consultants as well as sales people.  Give us a chance to prove ourselves and you will not regret the experience.  Our business has been built upon the referrals of our many satisfied customers.
