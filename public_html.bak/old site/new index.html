Jaeger Lumber has been serving our local community since 1937.  We are a family owned and operated business consisting of 7 yards.  We have evolved with the times to best serve our clients from being a home center to a builder-oriented lumberyard.  We pride ourselves in providing the high level of service and expertise the custom homebuilder and remodeler needs.

Jaeger Lumber’s employees are our greatest strength and most have been with us for many years.  We do not have the employee turnover of the chain stores and that is what keeps our customers coming back as well as our connection to the local community.  Our employees work for our customers as much as they work for Jaeger Lumber.  We are always searching for the best products in every category for our market areas.  We excel in providing the best millwork solutions to our builders and those that still have the time to “Do-It-Yourself”. We must stock the best quality to ensure our custom builders keep coming back.  Our hardware and tool departments have been designed for the professional builder where quality is a must.  

Our inventory mix is catered to our local market areas but we do ship out of alternate locations, when a product is not stocked locally.  We still consider ourselves a local lumberyard despite our growth and increasing market share.  We offer more than just the products we sell, we offer our experience.
